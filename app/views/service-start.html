Welcome to the pension options course

Learn your pensions options your own way

All the information you’ll get in an appointment has been collated here and brought together to help you learn about your pension options at a time, place and pace most convenient for you.



How it works

You can start and stop any time and if you get stuck or need to ask a question you can always give us a call or use our live chat service.

There are videos as well as text which cover the same information so you can choose which ever way feels most comfortable for you.

What you need to start

It will take you around 60 minutes to go through all the information, but you can go at your own pace so it could take more or less time.

To make the most of this course, it will help if you have these things to hand, but it’s not obligatory:

  • Your current defined contribution pot size and information from your pension provider
  • A statement showing ho wmuch state pension you’re expecting to get
  • An overview of any other sources of income you may have in retirement

We recommend that you take the time to understand all the options before making a decision to ensure you don’t end up paying more tax than needed.
